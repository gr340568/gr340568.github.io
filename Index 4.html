Various recipes for a fancy crustacean

There are a lot of ways to cook lobsters, such as steaming, roasting, boiling, and even using microwaves to cook a delicous lobster with. Most of these ways can be attempted at home for presenting to family members and friends. In this page, we will look at how lobster can be cooked in various ways.
